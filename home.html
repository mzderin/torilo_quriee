How it works

Get items of ANY size delivered in one piece, on time and on budget.

Quriee is an affordable and efficient same-day delivery platform. We get items delivered through drivers already headed towards your drop off location.

BUILT FOR YOU! WE DELIVER JUST ABOUT ANYTHING, ANYWHERE!

When you download the Quriee App you can choose your preferred delivery vehicle and we will connect you with a trusted driver already heading towards your delivery location, providing a faster and more flexible delivery option than traditional carriers and on-demand couriers.

EARN MONEY WHILE DRIVING ALONG YOUR ROUTE TODAY!

Get paid while driving to your destination. We understand the hustle so whether you want to earn some extra money for your daily needs, saving for something big,or you want to buy that gift to put a smile on your kid's face. Let's help you move closer to achieving that goal with Quriee today.
